Plant Power to the People

Our products contain plants with benefits. Like broad-spectrum cannabidiol, a.k.a. CBD.

Formula-First

We pair powerful ingredients - like vitamin C, squalene, hyaluronic acid, and bakuchiol - with broad-spectrum cannabidiol to help relieve a variety of skin issues and renew skin’s health.

Sensitive-Safe

CBD’s calming and anti-inflammatory properties help soothe skin without giving it extra stress

Third-Party Tested

Our organically grown broad-spectrum cannabidiol will always have a Certificate of Analysis by third-party labs, to ensure all the benefits of CBD without the high of THC.

How We Make it Matters

Giving you your best skin ever means using only the best stuff. The entire Luma & Leaf line is made with the highest quality, sustainably-sourced, clean ingredients. Formulated without parabens, sulfates, harmful alcohols, synthetic dyes, artificial fragrances, animal products, animal testing, and other bad stuff.
